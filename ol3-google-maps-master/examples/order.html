--- layout: example.html title: Order shortdesc: Example where layers can be re-ordered. docs: > In this example, layers are displayed in the table. They can be re-ordered by clicking the arrow buttons next to their names. The top-most layers will display in front of the layers placed below them in the table. The base Google layer is not affected by this, it will always display behind every layer. Vector layers will also always display in front of every layer while Google Maps is active. ---
