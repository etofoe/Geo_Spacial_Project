A simple map containing a single Google layer. OpenLayers is on top of Google Maps and controls all interactions on the map.

As soon as the OpenLayers map has a visible Google layer in it, the Google Maps map is activated by the library, which puts the OpenLayers map on top of the Google Maps one.
